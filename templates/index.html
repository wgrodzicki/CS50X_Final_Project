Instructions
Your beloved Forest has been infested by awful creatures from the North. You are the only one who can stand against the invadors.

Embrace your destiny, become The Eradicator and defend your homeland!

To control your character use:
  • left/right arrow keys to move
  • up arrow key to jump
  • spacebar to attack
Try to eradicate as many enemies as you can!
